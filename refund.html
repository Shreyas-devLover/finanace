Refund & Subscription Policy

Last Updated: April 10, 2025

1. Subscription Payments

All subscription payments made for tools, features, or services offered by Vinaiak Finance are final and non-refundable.

Please review the features and billing terms carefully before making any payments.

2. Workshop and Event Refunds

  • Requests for refunds will only be considered if submitted at least 48 hours prior to the scheduled event or workshop.
  • If a refund is approved, it will be processed within 20-30 business days.
  • Refunds are not available for missed sessions or partial attendance.

3. Batch Transfers and Substitutions

  • Participants may request a batch change or seat transfer to another individual up to 48 hours before the workshop starts.
  • If the new batch has a higher fee, the difference must be paid in advance. No refunds for lower-cost batches; a credit note may be issued instead.
  • Completion certificates will only be issued to the person who actually attends the full session.
